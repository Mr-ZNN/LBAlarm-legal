Privacy Policy

App: Alarm
Last updated: 2026-01-17

This Privacy Policy explains how Alarm (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles information. We respect your privacy and aim to be transparent.

Information We Collect

Alarm does not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, address, or contact list) for advertising or tracking purposes.

Data You Create in the App

The app may store user-created data (for example: alarms, settings, labels, schedules) to provide functionality. This data is stored:

  • Locally on your device, and/or
  • Synced via iCloud if you enable iCloud on your device (optional).

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment card details. Apple may provide us with non-identifying purchase information (such as whether a subscription is active) to enable premium features.

Analytics & Advertising

Alarm does not include third-party advertising SDKs. If analytics are used in the future, this policy will be updated to describe what is collected and how it is used.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing. Data may be shared only if required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

Children’s Privacy

Alarm is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You can delete the app to remove locally stored data.
  • If you use iCloud sync, you can manage iCloud data in your device settings.
